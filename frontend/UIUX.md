## üé® UI / UX Design System

ReLoop adopts a **Minimalist UI** design approach with subtle **Soft Glass (Frost UI)** accents to balance trust, clarity, and modern Web3 interaction.

The design prioritizes **readability, transparency, and real-world familiarity**, ensuring that non-crypto users (e.g. car buyers, dealers) can understand and use the product without friction.

---

### Design Philosophy

* **Trust-first design** for Real World Assets
* **Web2 familiarity** with Web3 logic hidden under the hood
* **Clarity over decoration**, especially for prices and payouts
* **Visual explanation of resale economics**, not financial jargon

The UI is intentionally calm and professional, reflecting the seriousness of ownership, resale, and value distribution.

---

### Design Style

**Primary Style:**

* Minimalist UI

**Secondary Accent:**

* Frost UI (Soft Glass) ‚Äî used sparingly

Soft glass effects are applied only to **informational layers**, such as modals and overlays, to help users understand resale flow without distracting from core actions.

---

### Color System

* **Primary Background:** Off-white / light neutral
* **Primary Text:** Dark charcoal / near-black
* **Accent Color:** Muted green (used to represent value flow and earnings)
* **Warning / Disclaimer:** Soft amber

The color palette is intentionally conservative to convey legitimacy and financial trust.

---

### Typography

* **Headings:** Inter / SF Pro / Satoshi
* **Body Text:** Inter
* **Numbers & Prices:** Monospaced variant for clarity

Typography hierarchy is emphasized to make prices, ownership status, and earnings immediately visible.

---

### What We Deliberately Avoid

* Token price charts
* APR / DeFi-style dashboards
* Neon colors or aggressive gradients
* Wallet-first onboarding
* Complex governance or DAO interfaces

ReLoop is a **resale marketplace**, not a trading terminal.

---

### UX Goal

A first-time user should be able to:

* Browse assets comfortably
* Understand resale value-sharing in under 30 seconds
* Trust the platform without knowing anything about blockchain

If the UI feels familiar, calm, and self-explanatory, the design has succeeded.
